About Us

Distributor for the Hotel & Hospitality Industry

Established in the Year 1982, Our Company was set up as a Distributor for the Hotel & Hospitality Industry with great plans for the future. Our clientele includes some of the largest hotels and restaurants in Oman. We engage our clients and ensure their expectations and requirements for F&B and OS&E are met with.

Our Product Range includes Crockery, Chinaware, Glassware, Barware, Kitchen Small-ware, Kitchen Equipment, Kitchen Utensils, In Room Electrical, In Room Equipment, Housekeeping Front of House, Catering Equipment, Stewarding Equipment, Banqueting Equipment etc!,Our clients recognise us for the Brands we Brands with, the extensive product range that we can offer and for our Service. Having over 42 years of experience, enables us to provide support to our client’s businesses.

Mission: Our goal is to give our clients exceptional customer service and value. Our strategy is to be distinct from other Distributors and offer the highest level of client assistance in helping them cut costs and be efficient.
